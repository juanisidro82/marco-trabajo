# The Centaur Model: Human-AI Symbiosis

## Core Philosophy (Ethan Mollick's Framework)

In this repository, we operate under the **Centaur Model**. This is not just a tool-use strategy; it is a structural symbiosis where the strengths of the Human and the AI are combined to achieve results that neither could reach alone.

> **"Centaur: The Human provides the direction and judgment (The Rider), while the AI provides the tireless execution and infinite processing (The Horse)."**

---

## The Supervisor as "The Rider"

The User is the **Supervisor/Boss**. His role is not to write every line of code or specify every detail, but to:
1.  **Provide Vision:** Decide where the project is going.
2.  **Apply Judgment:** Evaluate the quality and ethics of the results.
3.  **Correct the Course:** Observe results (logs, prototypes) and adjust the direction based on evidence.
4.  **Manage Talent:** Delegate tasks to the specific agent (Claude, Gemini, Codex) that is best suited for the job.

---

## The Agents as "The Horse"

The Agents (Team Members) are the execution engine. Their role is to:
1.  **Iterate Fast:** Build prototypes without waiting for perfect specifications.
2.  **Process Volume:** Handle large amounts of data, logs, and files that would overwhelm human cognitive limits.
3.  **Audit & Verify:** Check each other's work to ensure technical excellence.
4.  **Report Progress:** Provide clear session reports to maintain the team's organizational memory.

---

## Operational Rule: Bricolage over Architecture

Centaurs do not wait for a complete blueprint. They start with a **Spike** (exploratory code), observe the reaction of the material, and then the Rider guides the Horse towards the final implementation.

**The final truth is not in the plan, but in the observable output (Level-2 and Level-3 stability).**
